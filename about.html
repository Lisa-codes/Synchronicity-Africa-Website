Our Drive

At Synchronicity Africa, our drive is to create a sustainable impact in the communities we serve. We are fueled by a passion to empower individuals and transform lives through dedicated service and innovative solutions.

Our Values

Partnership

As a cornerstone of Synchroncity, we are fostering meaningful connections to unite efforts and amplify collective impact.

Wellness

We value mental, emotional, and physical well-being as a vital foundation for individuals, communities and collective progress of society.

Unity in Diversity

We celebrate diversity as a wellspring of strength, weaving a harmonious narrative of unity by embracing varied cultures, life perspectives and experiences..

Transformation

Through our Inspiring, Life changing Talk Shows, mentorship and mental Health Programs, we seek to instill resilience, tenacity as a catalyst for change and meaningful progress.

Growth & Empowerment

We nurture personal development, by helping individuals unlock untapped potential.

Sustainability

We wholeheartedly promote actions that leave a lasting, positive impact on individuals and overall wellbeing.

Our Co-Founders

Leonie Roessle, Co-Founder & Mental Health Coach

Leonie is a highly sought-after Mental Health Coach and Personal Development Trainer, with an intriguing background in market research and organizational development. With an unwavering desire to make a profound impact and a passion for empowering others, Leonie found her calling in coaching, and transitioned from corporate employment into the sphere of her own coaching business. Her intense drive to foster meaningful change, personal growth and to create substantial impact, has led her to expand her horizon beyond Germany, marking her footprint in Africa, specifically in Nairobi, Kenya. As a Coach and Personal Development Trainer she empowers organizations, schools and individuals with essential life skills and proven mental health tools, to flourish in their journeys towards holistic well-being, influencing lives from Europe to Africa. Guided by an unwavering vision to create a better world for all, where hope flourishes and dreams know no bounds, Leonie's path into co-founding Synchronicity was ignited by a profound commitment to help and uplift those in need. She fervently believes in the power of synchronized efforts to pave the way towards a brighter, more harmonious future for all..

David H Oumah, Co-Founder & Creative Director

Guided by the belief in life's mystical coincidences, and the undying desire to transform the lives of his Community; the idea for Synchronicity was born. David rose from humble beginnings in the slums, to become a beacon of hope and inspiration. His life took a transformative turn when he crossed path with two Amazing Souls, who would later impact his life and sponsor his University Education at one of the top International Universities in Africa. Fueled by gratitude and a newfound sense of purpose, David pursued International Business Administration setting the stage for an interesting career journey. He is currently a dynamic CEO and Founder of Kreative Hart Innovations; an Interior Design firm that creates and implements decor design concepts for malls, Events and Hotels in Nairobi. Guided by a deep desire to give back and create opportunities for others, he founded Synchronicity Africa, to be a catalyst for positive change, ( through meaningful Talkshows & Podcast) offering hope, compassion, and a pathway to a brighter future by reciprocating the life-changing Experiences he received. David envisions empowering others facing similar challenges by providing them with the chance to pursue education, a decent life away from poverty and to help many alike discover their true potential. .
